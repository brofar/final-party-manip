FF8 Final Party Manipulation Back to Garden

How to do the thing

This manipulation begins when the FMV after you defeat Adel begins playing.
Ensure that you're not entering any inputs and that turbo is off.
  1. As the party is falling, record Squall's first 12 movements.
  2. On the 12th movement, hold a direction on your controller for the duration of the cinematic.
    1. ADVANCED If you see Squall, progress RNG by letting go for n number of movements then resume holding a button.
  3. When the party's feet touch the ground at the end of the FMV, you can let go.
  4. At the final draw point of the castle, interact with it the number of times indicated by your result.
  5. Run to the final battle without stopping, and mash confirm at the door.
